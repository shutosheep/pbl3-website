Application Limitations

The vending machine app includes the following limitations:

  • Security:
    • The app does not include a login or registration system, which may pose limitations regarding the protection of users' personal information.
  • Limited Usage Area::
    • Currently, the app focuses on a small geographical area, specifically Shiga, Minamikusatsu. As a result, the app's usability is restricted to this region.
