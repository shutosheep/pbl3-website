Future Plans

  • Drink Rating and Reviewing:
    • Implement a feature that allows users to share their opinions and rate the drinks available in vending machines. This will enhance user engagement and provide valuable feedback to other users.
  • Expansion of Usage Area:
    • Extend the app's coverage beyond Minamikusatsu and include vending machines from various locations across Japan. This expansion will significantly increase the app's utility and user base.
  • Trash Can Location:
    • Add a feature that displays nearby trash can locations. This will encourage responsible waste disposal and improve user satisfaction.
  • Real-Time Data:
    • Integrate real-time data to inform users about the availability of beverages in vending machines. This will help users avoid disappointment when their desired drink is sold out.
